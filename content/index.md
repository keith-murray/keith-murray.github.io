Over the years, I've had ideas at varying levels of completeness. Some are polished, most are less than half-baked, but I think they all deserve to see the light of day. In my blog, there are two types of entries:

- Notes - Short, half-baked, ideas that I wrote for myself.
- Posts - Essays with an intended audience that have been through a few rounds of editing.

My main goal is to share with friends what I've been thinking about in the hopes of sparking a few conversations.

If you don't know me, checkout my [[aboutme.md | about me]] page.
