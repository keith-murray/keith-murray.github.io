At the bottom of the page you'll find links to my GitHub and LinkedIn pages. However, those platforms only scratch the surface of what people are like. To get a better idea of who I am, here are some facts about me:

- My middle name is Thomas, which comes from my grandfather, [Thomas Murray](https://www.ccee.iastate.edu/alumni-friends-industry-partners/hall-of-fame/thomas-m-murray/), who was a professor of structural engineering at Virginia Tech and a member of the National Academy of Engineers.
- I grew up in a small midwestern town of a little over 3,000 people in Southern Indiana.
- I spend my childhood participating in Boy Scouts, playing football, wrestling, and consuming video games. My favorite video game is [The Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Elder_Scrolls_III:_Morrowind).
- I gave up Boy Scouts, football, and wrestling to attend the [Indiana Academy for Science, Mathematics, and Humanities](https://academy.bsu.edu), a two-year public residential magnet school in Muncie, Indiana. That was the best decision I have ever made.

I'll admit, that's very confusing. Are all these photos of me? Indeed, most are. Let me explain:

- _top row, 3rd from the left_ - During the academic year 2024-2025, I pursued a Fulbright Scholarship in Budapest, Hungary. That photo is from an [article](https://wigner.hu/en/interview-keith-murray-fulbright-scholar-mit) my host institution, the Wigner Research Centre for Physics, wrote about me.
- _top row, 3rd from the right_ - Most people don't believe this image is of me, but it is. During the COVID-19 pandemic, my close friends and I lived in various Airbnbs around the US. Buisness Insider even wrote an [article mentioning our adventures](https://www.businessinsider.com/college-is-now-thousands-miles-away-from-campus-2020-11). During that time I grew out my hair. I don't think I'll make that mistake again.
- _top row, 2nd from the right_ - I walked on to the MIT Men's Heavyweight Rowing team during my freshman year, and subsequently rowed crew for all 4 undergraduate years. That photo is from the [Crew team's 2021-2022 roster](https://mitathletics.com/sports/mcrewhvy/roster/keith-murray/9961).
- _middle row, 2 leftmost images_ - I came to MIT on an Army ROTC scholarship and was a cadet during my freshman year. Those images are of me commisioning into the Army. ROTC did not agree with me, so I quit.
- _middle row, middle image_ - I was also a member of a fraternity, $\Phi \Delta \Theta$, while an undergrad at MIT. That photo is from when I pledged.
- _middle row, 2nd from the right_ - Guess. Is that me?[^1]
- _middle row, rightmost image_ - My good friend [Luke Igel](https://www.regressions.net) and I once gave a [talk about Cybernetics for TEDxMIT](https://youtu.be/shvaBUFduNM?feature=shared).

Currently I'm a Neuroscience PhD student at Princeton University interested in abstract reasoning and representation.
